# Communication Style

## Working with Howard (Internal)

- Bullet points and short paragraphs.
- Keep responses brief and direct.
- Casual tone.
- Never use emojis.
- Never use em dashes.

## Supplier & Business Communications

- Formal tone.
- Professional and concise.
- No slang or filler language.

## Customer Communications

- Match the tone and voice of the target audience for each brand.
- Helmet Head: practical, discreet buyers who value functionality.
- Petoku: pet owners who care about their dog's health and wellness.
- Azen Garden: plant enthusiasts and home gardeners.

## Amazon Listings

- Write for the target customer, not for Howard.
- Lead with the benefit, not the feature.
- Integrate high-search keywords naturally.
- Follow Amazon listing best practices: title, bullet points, description, backend keywords.
